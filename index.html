Clinical Scope

Conditions We Treat

Our orthodontist-approved shape-memory polymer aligners apply targeted, continuous force to solve a wide range of malocclusions.

Deep Bite

Upper front teeth overlap lower front teeth excessively, which can cause wear, joint pain, and jaw alignment issues.

AMP™ Solution

Optimized vertical intrusion force to level the arches and restore healthy guidance.

Open Bite

Front upper and lower teeth do not overlap or touch when closed, making chewing and speaking difficult.

AMP™ Solution

Continuous, elastic force profiles designed to extrude front teeth and close the bite naturally.

Crowding

Overlapping, twisted, or misaligned teeth due to insufficient jaw space, making cleaning difficult.

AMP™ Solution

Controlled expansion and sequential rotations to align teeth neatly into the arch.

Spacing

Gaps or spaces between teeth caused by bone-to-tooth discrepancies, causing food entrapment.

AMP™ Solution

Broad bodily translation movements to close gaps and optimize tooth contact points.

Crossbite

One or more upper teeth bite inside the lower teeth, leading to uneven tooth wear and asymmetric jaw growth.

AMP™ Solution

Transverse expansion forces to move upper teeth outward into proper occlusion.

Underbite

Lower front teeth sit in front of the upper front teeth when closed, placing extra strain on jaw joints.

AMP™ Solution

Anteroposterior movements combined with elastic attachments for skeletal/dental correction.

Inside The Lab

How Your Aligners Are Made

From your scan to your clinic — every aligner is precision-crafted in our state-of-the-art facility

01

3D Scanning

Intraoral scans or physical models are digitized with precision to create an accurate 3D map of each patient's dentition.

→
02

3D Printing

Each aligner stage model is printed using medical-grade resin with micron-level accuracy on our industrial 3D printers.

→
03

Thermoforming

Our biocompatible shape-memory polymer sheets are vacuum-formed over each printed model under precise heat and pressure.

→
04

Precision Trimming

Each aligner is trimmed and finished by our lab technicians to clinical-grade margins — smooth, comfortable, and precise.

→
05

Packing & Delivery

Aligners are individually labelled, hygienically sealed, and packed in premium pouches — dispatched directly to your clinic.
